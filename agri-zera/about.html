Who are We?

All problems one solution- Agri-Zera APP.

One of the biggest problem that farmers are facing now a days is lack of awareness, so to overcome this problem, we must begin by making them more aware about the sources and resources available to them and hence for making them aware we have decided that our application will contain everything from theoretical explanations to tutorials videos. For example, to enhance our user's knowledge, our app will include lectures and tutorials on use of bio fuels and manures.

It will explain to them the usage and will also have purchasing facility. We will include all links, pages and necessary contacts at our tutorial section. We will also include lectures on the necessity of alternate energy like wind, solar and hydroelectric energy.
